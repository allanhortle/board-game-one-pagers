## Setup

1. Arrange the starting tiles as instructed in the chosen scenario.
2. Place pawns on their respective starting locations based on their color.
3. Shuffle the remaining tiles and prepare them as a draw pile.
4. Place the hourglass timer next to the board.
5. Assign action tiles to players.

## Player's turn

* This game is played simultaneously with no designated turn order.
* Players may only perform actions assigned to their action tiles (e.g., move north, use escalators).
* Players communicate silently unless the timer is flipped.

## Scoring

There is no scoring system in Magic Maze. You win or lose as a team.

## Game end

The game ends when:
* All pawns successfully exit the shopping mall (win).
* The timer runs out (lose).
